# AgeCraft next objective: playtest-ready pre-alpha

## Objective
Turn the current 3-era AgeCraft MVP prototype into a **playtest-ready pre-alpha** that can honestly validate:
- whether the crafting loop is fun enough to repeat
- whether bottlenecks create meaningful craft-vs-buy decisions
- whether creator loyalty and lineage make the market feel socially/economically interesting
- whether the mobile-first experience is understandable without live guidance

## Success criteria
A new external tester should be able to:
1. open the build without setup confusion
2. understand what to do in under 1 minute
3. play through the 3-era run without getting semantically lost
4. feel at least one meaningful decision between self-crafting and buying
5. notice that creator identity / loyalty matters
6. finish with a clear sense of what improved or frustrated them

## Phase rule
This phase is not about adding full blockchain reality yet.
It is about proving gameplay and economy semantics with a real shareable prototype.

## Exit condition
This phase is complete when:
- pacing and game feel are tuned enough for outside playtests
- mobile onboarding/feedback is clearer
- a shareable web playtest build exists
- the next iteration plan is grounded in actual tester feedback rather than speculation
